ANJUMAN MUFIDUL ISLAM

JR TOWER

52 LAKE CIRCUS

>> go to images

The beauty of a clever design scheme lies in its purity. The principle aim of this scheme is only to preserve and perpetuate purity in its simplest form as the image of Anjuman Mufidul Islam

The name "Anjuman Mufidul Islam (AMI)" stands for an organization that serves mankind. It stands for an image or a figure that represents shelter for people in need. It is a place where people can go whenever they are in distress. So, the building for AMI should also stand for the society and people; a building that does not give any sense of restriction. The building must have a very high level of acceptance to people. So the relation of the building to the adjacent settings must be well sought. But the building is not entirely dedicated to AMI functions. It also houses rentable commercial floors, where the ratio between the .1 floor spaces and other commercial activities is approximately 1:4. Here comes the conflict in approach -spirit of .1 & demand of a highly valuable commercial space.
