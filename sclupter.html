The beauty of an intelligent design scheme lies in its invisibility, this core idea woris behind the formulation of each design by Cubeinside team. Sculpture House in Gazipur, this vacation house is designed fora family of 7 people which is located at Islampur, Gazipur -a sub urban/industrial area near Dhaka.

The 4 acre site has a plenty to offer fora placing a vacation hcuse, where the final location of the building mass was selected in such a way that it could get the highest exposure to the Ptrionicarl concern vacation house having a lot of sculptures around it to make the line thinner between reality and imagination.
